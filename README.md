🎮 KURUNYA

A Simple Endless Runner Game 🦖 (AARUNYA)

KURUNYA is a lightweight, fun browser game inspired by the classic runner style.
Jump, avoid obstacles, and score as high as you can!

---

🚀 Live Demo

👉 https://0xzro.github.io/kurunya-game/

---

🎯 Gameplay

- 🍑 = Player (AARUNYA)
- 💜 = Obstacle
- Press Space or Tap to jump
- Avoid hitting the obstacle
- Score increases over time

---

✨ Features

- 🎮 Simple and addictive gameplay
- 📱 Mobile + Desktop support
- ⚡ Fast and lightweight
- 🧠 Easy controls (tap / space)
- 🏆 Live score tracking

📦 How to Play

1. Open the game
2. Tap screen or press space to jump
3. Avoid 💜 obstacles
4. Try to beat your high score
